# Installation
---

To install Hyper Megaphone it's quite simple head over to the [KeyMaster](https://keymaster.fivem.net/) and go to granted assets and find the one called `Hyper Megaphone` by `Hyperscale`.
Simply download that and put it into your server directory.

And thats it, Your done!

If you require assistance installing this resource join our [discord](https://pribilovic.net/discord) and open a ticket! We will be more than happy to help.
